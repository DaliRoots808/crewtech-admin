As an admin / crew lead, you agree to:

  • Use CrewTech only for legitimate work scheduling purposes.

As a worker, you agree to:

  • Review job details carefully before confirming shifts.
  • Use your personal worker link only for yourself and not share it publicly.
  • Notify your lead if you believe any job details or your contact information are incorrect.

4. SMS messages and worker links

CrewTech uses SMS (text messages) to send workers:

  • Job invitations and shift details
  • Confirmations when a worker accepts or declines a shift
  • Personal dashboard links so workers can view their own upcoming jobs

Message frequency depends on how often your lead schedules you. Message and data rates may apply, according to your mobile carrier plan.

Workers can usually stop receiving job-related SMS from a specific sender by replying “STOP” to a message from that sender. Workers may also contact the crew lead directly and/or email us at crewtechteam@gmail.com to raise any concerns.

For more detail on how we handle SMS and data, please review our Privacy & SMS Policy.
