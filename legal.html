CrewTech Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

CrewTech (“we,” “our,” “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of workers, team leads, and clients who use our scheduling and communication platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Worker Data: Names, phone numbers, and job assignments.
  • Job Information: Job names, dates, times, locations, notes, and worker assignments.
  • Communication Data: SMS confirmations (YES/NO responses), timestamps, delivery records.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, and basic analytics.

2. How We Use Information

  • To send job invites, confirmations, and updates through SMS.
  • To maintain worker availability and scheduling dashboards.
  • To improve and maintain platform functionality.
  • To provide customer support to crew leads.

3. Data Retention

Job and worker information is stored only in the browser of the user operating the CrewTech admin tool, unless explicitly backed up or exported. Worker SMS details are transmitted securely through the SMS provider but not stored by CrewTech.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties except:

  • Our SMS provider solely for message delivery.
  • As required by law or court order.

5. Your Rights

Workers may request removal of their data by contacting their CrewTech team lead.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email: crewtechteam@gmail.com

SMS Policy & Disclosures

CrewTech uses SMS messages to communicate job invites, confirmations, schedule updates, and job reminders to workers. By providing your phone number to a team lead using CrewTech, you consent to receiving these messages.

1. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on job activity but typically includes:

  • Job invite messages
  • Confirmation reminders
  • Schedule updates if a job changes

2. Opt-Out / Stop Messages

Workers may opt out at any time by replying:

  • STOP – to stop all messages
  • HELP – to receive support info

No further messages will be sent after opting out unless the worker opts back in.

3. Message & Data Rates

Your carrier’s standard message and data rates may apply.

4. Supported Carriers

All major U.S. carriers are supported.

5. Privacy of SMS Data

SMS logs (e.g., delivery and confirmation) are handled securely through our SMS provider and are not sold or shared.

6. Contact for SMS Assistance

For SMS help, reply HELP to any message or email: crewtechteam@gmail.com
